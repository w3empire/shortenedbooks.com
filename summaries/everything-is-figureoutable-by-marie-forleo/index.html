Everything is Figureoutable by Marie Forleo (Summary & Review)

author Dean Stewart

“Everything is Figureoutable” is a self-help book written by Marie Forleo and published in 2019. The book’s central premise is that with the right mindset and approach, anything can be figured out, and any goal can be achieved.

Forleo draws on her own experiences, as well as those of people she has worked with, to illustrate the power of the “figureoutable” mindset. She provides practical tools and exercises to help readers develop this mindset and apply it to their own lives.

The book covers a wide range of topics, from overcoming fear and self-doubt to building resilience and taking action towards one’s goals. Forleo emphasizes the importance of taking action and not letting perfectionism or fear hold you back.

“Everything is Figureoutable” has been well-received by readers and has been praised for its practical advice and inspiring message. It is a useful resource for anyone looking to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.

Idea 1: Everything is figureoutable: The central idea of the book is that with the right mindset and approach, anything can be figured out.

Idea 2: Excuses hold us back: Often, we use excuses like “I don’t have enough time” or “I’m not smart enough” to avoid taking action towards our goals. Forleo encourages us to identify and overcome these excuses.

Idea 3: Fear is normal: Fear is a natural part of the human experience, but it doesn’t have to hold us back. Forleo provides tools and techniques to overcome fear and take action towards our goals.

Idea 4: Progress, not perfection: Perfectionism can be a major obstacle to success. Forleo encourages us to focus on progress rather than perfection and to embrace the process of learning and growth.

Idea 5: Mindset matters: Our mindset can have a significant impact on our ability to succeed. Forleo encourages us to cultivate a growth mindset and to approach challenges as opportunities for learning and growth.

Idea 6: Taking action is key: To achieve our goals, we must take consistent action. Forleo provides practical tips and strategies to help us overcome procrastination and take action towards our goals.

Idea 7: Failure is an opportunity: Failure is not the end of the road but an opportunity for learning and growth. Forleo encourages us to embrace failure as a natural part of the process and to use it as a stepping stone towards success.

Idea 8: Self-care is important: Taking care of ourselves is essential for achieving our goals. Forleo emphasizes the importance of self-care and provides tips and strategies for prioritizing our physical and mental well-being.

Idea 9: Surround yourself with support: Our environment and the people we surround ourselves with can have a significant impact on our success. Forleo encourages us to seek out positive, supportive relationships and to distance ourselves from negative influences.

Idea 10: Persistence pays off: Success is often the result of persistence and perseverance. Forleo encourages us to stay committed to our goals and to keep pushing forward even when things get tough.
