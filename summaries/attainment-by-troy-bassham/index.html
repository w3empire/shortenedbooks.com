Attainment by Troy Bassham (Summary & Review)

author Jared Wright
Rating

“Attainment: The 12 Elements of Elite Performance” is a book written by Troy Bassham, a renowned sports psychologist and coach. The book explores the key elements necessary for achieving peak performance in any field.

Drawing from his extensive experience working with elite athletes, Bassham presents a comprehensive guide that goes beyond traditional approaches to success.

Bassham’s main intention with this book is to provide readers with practical strategies and insights to unlock their full potential and excel in their chosen endeavors. By examining the 12 essential elements of elite performance, he aims to help individuals develop the mindset, skills, and habits necessary to consistently perform at the highest level.

The book’s main themes revolve around the importance of mental conditioning, goal-setting, focus, and self-belief. Bassham emphasizes the role of the mind in achieving success, highlighting the power of visualization, self-talk, and resilience.

He presents a holistic approach to performance, addressing not only the technical and physical aspects but also the mental and emotional dimensions.

Element 1: Clarity of Vision

In the first element, Bassham emphasizes the significance of having a clear and compelling vision of one’s goals. He argues that a well-defined vision provides direction, motivation, and a sense of purpose.

By visualizing the desired outcome and setting specific goals, individuals can align their actions and decisions accordingly. Bassham encourages readers to define their aspirations with clarity, considering both short-term and long-term objectives.

To illustrate this idea, Bassham shares the story of an Olympic athlete who, through a clear vision of winning a gold medal, fueled his commitment, perseverance, and daily training regimen. The athlete’s unwavering focus on his vision propelled him to overcome setbacks and achieve remarkable success.

Element 2: Self-Belief

The second element explores the crucial role of self-belief in elite performance. Bassham emphasizes the importance of cultivating a strong belief in one’s abilities, even in the face of challenges or doubts. He argues that self-belief acts as a powerful driver of motivation, resilience, and the willingness to push beyond perceived limitations.

Bassham provides practical strategies for strengthening self-belief, such as affirmations, positive self-talk, and building a supportive network of individuals who believe in one’s potential. He also highlights the need to acknowledge and challenge self-limiting beliefs that may hinder progress.

Bassham uses the example of a professional golfer who, despite early setbacks and doubts, developed unwavering self-belief through persistent practice and a growth-oriented mindset. This shift in belief allowed the golfer to overcome obstacles and ultimately achieve remarkable success in the sport.

Element 3: Emotional Mastery

The third element delves into the importance of emotional mastery for elite performance. Bassham emphasizes the need to understand and manage emotions effectively to perform at one’s best.

He explores various techniques, including mindfulness, breath control, and emotional regulation, to help individuals navigate the emotional roller coaster that often accompanies high-pressure situations.

Bassham illustrates the significance of emotional mastery through the example of a professional musician who learned to channel nervousness and anxiety into focused energy during performances. By cultivating emotional awareness and adopting strategies to regulate emotions, the musician was able to enhance creativity, concentration, and overall performance.

Element 4: Laser Focus

The fourth element focuses on the power of laser-like focus and concentration. Bassham asserts that elite performers possess the ability to direct their attention fully on the task at hand, minimizing distractions and maximizing productivity. He explores techniques such as visualization, mental rehearsal, and mindfulness practices to enhance focus and improve performance outcomes.

Bassham shares the story of an Olympic swimmer who honed her ability to maintain laser focus by incorporating visualization exercises into her training routine. Through vividly imagining successful races and visualizing each stroke, she developed a heightened sense of focus, leading to improved performance and record-breaking achievements.

Element 5: Resilience and Adaptability

The fifth element highlights the importance of resilience and adaptability in the face of adversity. Bassham acknowledges that setbacks and challenges are inevitable on the journey to elite performance, but it is one’s ability to bounce back and adapt that distinguishes top performers.

Bassham introduces strategies to enhance resilience, such as reframing failure as feedback, embracing a growth mindset, and cultivating a support system. He emphasizes the need to view setbacks as opportunities for learning and growth, ultimately leading to improved performance.

The story of an entrepreneur serves as an illustration of resilience and adaptability. Despite encountering numerous business failures, the entrepreneur remained persistent, adaptable, and open to new opportunities. This mindset shift allowed the entrepreneur to eventually achieve significant success and make a positive impact in their industry.

Element 6: Discipline and Consistency

The sixth element delves into the importance of discipline and consistency in elite performance. Bassham argues that consistent practice, dedication, and adherence to routines are essential for long-term success. He explores strategies for developing discipline, such as setting clear goals, establishing structured routines, and embracing deliberate practice.

Bassham shares the story of a professional tennis player who achieved exceptional results through disciplined training, consistent practice, and adherence to a well-defined routine. The player’s commitment to daily practice sessions, physical conditioning, and mental preparation contributed significantly to their rise to the top ranks of the sport.

Element 7: Continuous Learning and Improvement

The seventh element emphasizes the value of continuous learning and improvement in the pursuit of elite performance. Bassham encourages individuals to adopt a growth mindset and embrace a lifelong journey of acquiring new knowledge, skills, and perspectives. He explores strategies such as seeking feedback, engaging in deliberate practice, and embracing challenges as opportunities for growth.

An example cited by Bassham is the story of a successful CEO who attributed their achievements to a commitment to continuous learning and improvement. Through voracious reading, attending conferences, and seeking out mentors, the CEO developed a breadth of knowledge and a versatile skill set that contributed to their sustained success.

Element 8: Effective Goal Setting

The eighth element focuses on the art of effective goal-setting. Bassham argues that well-structured and challenging goals provide direction, focus, and motivation. He presents a framework for setting goals that are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART). Bassham emphasizes the importance of setting both outcome goals and process goals to drive progress and maintain motivation.

Bassham shares the story of an athlete who set a specific goal of completing a marathon within a particular time frame. By breaking down the goal into smaller milestones, creating a detailed training plan, and tracking progress, the athlete was able to stay motivated and focused, ultimately achieving the desired outcome.

Element 9: Visualization and Mental Rehearsal

The ninth element explores the power of visualization and mental rehearsal in elite performance. Bassham explains how vividly imagining successful performances and mentally rehearsing each step can enhance confidence, focus, and overall performance. He provides practical techniques for incorporating visualization into daily practice routines.

Bassham illustrates the impact of visualization through the example of a professional dancer who utilized mental rehearsal to improve her technique and artistic expression. By mentally practicing routines, envisioning flawless performances, and visualizing every intricate movement, the dancer developed a heightened sense of muscle memory and precision.

Element 10: Strategic Planning and Execution

The tenth element focuses on strategic planning and execution. Bassham emphasizes the importance of developing a clear plan, breaking it down into actionable steps, and executing with purpose and determination. He explores techniques such as prioritization, time management, and accountability to ensure effective execution of goals.

Bassham shares the story of a business executive who achieved remarkable success by meticulously planning each day, setting priorities, and executing tasks with unwavering focus. Through strategic planning and disciplined execution, the executive was able to consistently achieve desired outcomes and make significant progress in their career.

Element 11: Effective Communication and Collaboration

The eleventh element explores the significance of effective communication and collaboration in achieving elite performance. Bassham emphasizes the importance of clear and concise communication, both within oneself and with others. He highlights the value of building strong relationships, fostering teamwork, and leveraging collective strengths to enhance performance outcomes.

Bassham provides strategies for effective communication, such as active listening, constructive feedback, and cultivating a culture of open and honest communication. He emphasizes the power of collaboration in generating innovative ideas, solving complex problems, and achieving collective success.

The tale of a successful team in a corporate environment is one example that Bassham uses. Through effective communication channels, regular team meetings, and a shared vision, the team was able to achieve remarkable results by capitalizing on the diverse skills and perspectives of its members.

Element 12: Purpose and Meaning

The twelfth and final element focuses on the importance of purpose and meaning in elite performance. Bassham argues that individuals who connect their performance to a deeper sense of purpose and meaning are more likely to achieve sustained success and fulfillment. He explores techniques for uncovering personal values, aligning goals with purpose, and creating a meaningful impact in one’s chosen field.

Bassham shares stories of individuals who discovered their purpose and integrated it into their performance. These individuals found greater motivation, satisfaction, and resilience in their pursuit of excellence. By infusing their performance with a sense of purpose, they were able to make a positive difference in their lives and the lives of others.

Who is this Book Recommended For?

This book is recommended for individuals who are driven to achieve elite performance in any field. Athletes, entrepreneurs, artists, and professionals seeking to unlock their full potential and enhance their performance will find valuable insights and practical strategies within this book.

The book is also relevant for coaches, mentors, and leaders who work with individuals or teams striving for excellence. The principles and concepts presented in “Attainment” can be applied to guide and support others in their pursuit of peak performance.

Review of the Book

“Attainment: The 12 Elements of Elite Performance” offers a comprehensive and practical guide to achieving peak performance. Troy Bassham’s extensive experience as a sports psychologist and coach shines through as he presents a well-structured framework and valuable strategies for excellence.

The book excels in providing actionable steps and practical exercises that readers can implement immediately. Each element is thoroughly explored, with clear explanations and relevant examples that help readers understand the concepts and see their application in real-life scenarios.

One aspect that could be improved is the depth of exploration for each element. While Bassham provides valuable insights and strategies, some readers may crave more in-depth analysis or case studies. Plus, the book primarily draws examples from the world of sports, which may limit its appeal to individuals outside of athletic pursuits.

Conclusion

“Attainment: The 12 Elements of Elite Performance” by Troy Bassham is a good read for individuals seeking to achieve peak performance in their chosen field. Through a comprehensive exploration of the essential elements of elite performance, Bassham provides readers with practical strategies, exercises, and insights to unlock their full potential.

The book’s emphasis on the mental and emotional dimensions of performance sets it apart from traditional self-help or performance guides. By delving into topics such as self-belief, emotional mastery, and resilience, Bassham acknowledges the holistic nature of achievement and offers readers a well-rounded approach to success.

In short, “Attainment” is a powerful guide that encourages readers to adopt a growth mindset, embrace continuous learning, and cultivate the necessary habits and mindset for elite performance. Whether one aspires to excel in sports, business, the arts, or any other endeavor, this book serves as a valuable resource for unlocking one’s full potential and achieving extraordinary results.
