How To Be A 3% Man by Corey Wayne (Summary & Review)

author Mark Wheeler

“How To Be A 3% Man, Winning The Heart Of The Woman Of Your Dreams” is a self-help book written by Corey Wayne that focuses on dating and relationships. The book aims to provide men with the knowledge and skills they need to attract and keep the woman of their dreams.

The title refers to the idea that only 3% of men are successful in their relationships with women, and the author aims to help readers become part of this exclusive group.

The book is divided into 15 chapters, covering topics such as understanding women, building attraction, communication, and the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. The author draws on his own experiences and those of his clients to provide practical advice on how to improve your dating and relationship skills.

While the book has received mixed reviews, with some readers finding it helpful and others criticizing it for its simplistic approach and use of gender stereotypes, it has become popular among men seeking dating and relationship advice.

It is important to note that while the book may offer useful insights, it is not a one-size-fits-all solution, and individual results may vary depending on personal circumstances and the dynamics of each relationship.

Idea 1: Be masculine: According to the author, women are attracted to masculine energy, so it’s important for men to embrace their masculinity and be confident in their approach.

Idea 2: Understand women: The author emphasizes the importance of understanding women’s emotions and psychology in order to be successful in dating and relationships.

Idea 3: Build attraction: The book provides specific strategies for building attraction, such as being playful, teasing, and being a challenge.

Idea 4: Communicate effectively: Effective communication is key to building and maintaining relationships. The author provides tips for communicating in a way that is authentic and respectful.

Idea 5: Be a leader: The author stresses the importance of being a leader in your relationship, taking charge and making decisions.

Idea 6: Don’t be needy: Neediness is unattractive, so it’s important for men to have their own life and interests outside of the relationship.

Idea 7: Maintain healthy boundaries: Boundaries are important in any relationship, and the author provides guidance on setting and enforcing healthy boundaries.

Idea 8: Take care of yourself: The author emphasizes the importance of taking care of your physical and mental health in order to be a good partner.

Idea 9: Understand the dating process: The book provides a detailed explanation of the dating process, from attraction to commitment, and provides guidance on how to navigate each stage successfully.

Idea 10: Focus on long-term happiness: The ultimate goal of the book is to help men find long-term happiness in their relationships by developing healthy habits and behaviors.
